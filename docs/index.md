# PomoBar Documentation

**A minimalist Pomodoro timer for Obsidian that keeps you focused on what matters.**

PomoBar integrates the Pomodoro Technique directly into your Obsidian workflow, helping you maintain productive work sessions without ever leaving your notes. The timer lives in your status bar‚Äîunobtrusive yet always visible‚Äîso you can stay on track without distractions.

### üñ±Ô∏è Intuitive Controls

Manage your timers with simple mouse interactions:

- **Left-click** - Start or pause the timer
- **Middle-click** - Cycle between work, short break, and long break
- **Right-click** - Reset the current timer

- Start/Pause timer
- Reset current timer
- Cycle to next timer duration
- Toggle sound notifications
- Toggle status bar visibility
- Toggle icon visibility

### üîÑ Automated Flow

Optional auto-progression automatically starts the next timer in your Pomodoro cycle, or pause after each session for manual control‚Äîwhatever works best for your workflow.

### ‚öôÔ∏è Fully Customizable

- Auto-progress behavior
